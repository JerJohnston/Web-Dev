DuckTales the Movie: Treasure of the Lost Lamp

The Synopsis

DuckTales is initially an American tv series, the movie, DuckTales The Movie takes place between seasons three and four and was released August 3rd, 1990. The story begins in the Middle East where Scrooge McDuck is accompanined by Huey, Dewey, Louie, Webby Vanderquack and Launchpad McQuack off in search of a recently discovered treasure chest. Only to be initially disappointed by the chest only contained clothes, however upon Scrooges search he found an old map. Guided by the theif Dijon the group sets out the find the treasure.
